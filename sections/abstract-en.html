Abstract

The risk ontology is a structured method for knowldge representation to support context-oriented risk identification when identifying the risk factors of onshore wind farm project

The suggested prefix for the CDRIO ontology is cdrio

The Turtle version of the CDRIO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cdrio/cdrio.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the CDRIO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cdrio/cdrio.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the CDRIO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cdrio/cdrio.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the CDRIO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cdrio/cdrio.nt

The CDRIO ontology was created by Emad Mohamed (University of Alberta) during his PhD research (2017-2021) supported by Future Energy Systems research as part of the Canada First Research Excellent Fund (CFREF FES-T11-P01).
